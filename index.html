4. Feature Specifications & Sections
How to check: Cross-reference the point callouts on the Levels Map with the Specification Tables on the Section Sheets.

• Excavation Depths: Verify the "EXCAVATE -X.X m" map callout for a feature matches the "MAX CUT DEPTH" in its table (e.g., Scrape 1 should match 0.3-0.5m).
• Finish Levels: Ensure AOD targets on the map exactly match the "FINISH LEVEL" columns in the corresponding table (e.g., Riffle 1 levels matching 127.8 m AOD).
How to check: Review the four level columns for Riffles (UST Baseline, DST Baseline, UST Finish, DST Finish). Ensure that for any single feature, the downstream (DST) levels are lower than or equal to the upstream (UST) levels.
How to check: Compare the finish levels of multiple Riffles in series. A downstream feature (e.g., Riffle 2) cannot have a higher finish level than the feature immediately upstream of it (e.g., Riffle 1).
How to check: Review the "MAX CUT DEPTH" in the specification tables or calculate the difference between the existing LiDAR levels and proposed cut levels on the map. Flag any depths that appear excessive (e.g., over 1.5m - 2.0m) to confirm they are intentional and structurally safe.
